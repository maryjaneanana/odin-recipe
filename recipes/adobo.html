Chiken Adobo

Description

Chicken Adobo is a classic Filipino dish known for its savory, tangy flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken (thighs, drumsticks, or a mix)
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vinegar (white or apple cider vinegar)
  • 1 cup water or chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 4-5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon whole black peppercorns
  • 1-2 tablespoons brown sugar (optional, for a touch of sweetness)
  • 1 tablespoon salt (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1-2 potatoes (optional, peeled and cubed)
  • 1-2 carrots (optional, peeled and sliced)
  • 1-2 tablespoons cooking oil (for frying potatoes and carrots, if using)

Steps:

  1. Marinate the Chicken
  2. Prepare the Potatoes and Carrots (optional)
  3. Sear the Chicken
  4. Cook the Adobo
  5. Simmer
  6. Adjust Seasoning
  7. Serve
